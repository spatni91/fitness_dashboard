VO2 Max Intervals
4×4 protocol · ~46 min total

Norwegian 4×4

THE benchmark protocol for raising VO2 max — treadmill, bike, or rower
10 min easy warm-up. Then 4 rounds of 4 min hard (90–95% max HR, "comfortably unsustainable past 4 min") + 3 min active recovery (60–70% max HR). Finish with 6 min cool-down. Track your hard-interval pace/incline and HR to see VO2max trending up over weeks.

● Cardio — Day 4
Threshold Tempo Run
Sustained effort · ~50 min total

Tempo / Lactate Threshold

Builds the engine that lets you sustain hard pace longer — treadmill, bike, rower, or outdoors
10 min easy warm-up. Then 30 min continuous at threshold pace (~80–85% max HR — "hard but conversational in short bursts"). 10 min easy cool-down. If 30 min straight is too much early on, split into 2×15 min with 3 min easy between.
